Biography

I am now a final-year PhD student at School of Computer Science and Engineering at Nanyang Technological University, under supervision of Professor Miao Chunyan. Before that, I received Bachelor degree from college of software, Jilin university, and Master degree from Institute of computing technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences, advised by Professor Zhang Yongdong and Associate Professor Gao Ke.

My research interests include explainable artificial intelligence (XAI) (Git Repo) on healthcare and medical image computing. Recently I am working on the counterfactual explanations , and robustness/security of counterfactual explanations .

I am open and welcome to collaborate with researchers, who are interested in XAI. Feel free to drop me an email.
